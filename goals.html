SMART Goals Framework

A proven method for setting effective goals

S
Specific

What exactly do I want to accomplish?

Example: "Learn Spanish" → "Have a 30-minute conversation in Spanish about daily topics"

M
Measurable

How will I measure success?

Example: "Improve fitness" → "Run 5km in under 30 minutes"

A
Achievable

Is this goal realistic with available resources?

Example: Consider time, skills, and resources needed

R
Relevant

Does this align with my broader objectives?

Example: "How does this goal fit into my long-term plans?"

T
Time-bound

What is the deadline for completion?

Example: "By December 31, 2024" or "Within 3 months"
