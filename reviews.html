Tips for Effective Reviews

Be Consistent

Schedule regular review sessions and stick to them. Consistency builds the habit.

Be Honest

Honest self-reflection leads to genuine growth. Don't sugarcoat your challenges.

Celebrate Wins

Always acknowledge your achievements, no matter how small. Celebration fuels motivation.

Focus on Action

Every review should result in specific, actionable next steps for improvement.
