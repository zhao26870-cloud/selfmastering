Habit Building Tips

Start Small

Begin with 2-3 minute versions of your habits. Small wins build momentum and make habits stick.

Habit Stacking

Attach new habits to existing routines. "After [current habit], I will [new habit]."

Don't Break the Chain

Visualize your streak. The longer it gets, the more motivated you'll be to keep it going.

Accountability

Share your progress with friends or join a community. Social accountability increases success rates.
